About Javan Corp.

Javan Corp. is your trusted partner in safe and stylish van rentals. Founded with a passion for quality service and community trust, we provide top-notch vehicles and friendly drivers to get you where you need to go — safely, affordably, and on time.

Whether you're heading to a family reunion, a business trip, or a group outing, Javan Corp. ensures a smooth ride from start to finish.

Meet Our Founders

JAVAN FriendGroup

Founders & CEO's

Javan Corp. was founded in 2024, sparked by a light-hearted joke among friends about going to Japan. Realizing they didn’t have enough money to make the trip, they turned that dream into motivation. They decided to build a business that could help them save for their goal. That's how the idea of a van rental service was born—cleverly named "JA-VAN" as a fusion of "Japan" and "Van." What began as a joke is now a growing company, driven by passion, friendship, and purpose.
